# n8n Life Sciences Sales Automations

A curated collection of n8n workflows for automating sales and marketing processes in the **life sciences industry** — pharma, biotech, CRO, HEOR, and medtech.

All workflows are sanitised (no personal credentials), fully documented, and ready to import into any n8n instance.

## Who this is for

These workflows are built for sales and marketing professionals working in:

- **Pharma & Biotech** — BD outreach, pipeline communications, KOL engagement
- **HEOR & Market Access** — Reaching payer teams, HTA bodies, outcomes research leads
- **CROs** — Business development and proposal follow-up automation
- **Medtech** — Product launch outreach, clinical specialist engagement

---

## How to use any workflow

1. Download the `.json` file from the relevant folder
2. In n8n, go to **Workflows → Import → Upload JSON**
3. Follow the setup instructions in the folder's `README.md`
4. Add your own credentials (Google Sheets, Gmail, LinkedIn, etc.)
5. Activate and run
